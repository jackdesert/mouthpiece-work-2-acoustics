SUBJECT: New Orleans
Sharon and I were forcibly evacuated by troops yesterday and are now 
with family in Birmingham, Alabama. We were allowed to take NOTHING 
with us, so all we have is the clothes on our back. We had flooding on 
the first floor in the repair shop, the showroom, the shipping room, 
and the office. The warehouse we had next door seems to have floated 
off its foundation and was partially collapsed when we left. All 
survivivng inventory is still in New Orleans. We have nothing with us.
We don't expect to be able to reopen for at least six weeks. We don't 
know what the looters will leave, so we don't know what will be there 
when we get back. The Army is going from door to door opening houses 
and looking for bodies, so we expect to have everything left wide open. 
They were begining this process in our neighborhood as we were being 
loaded onto the truck that took us away.

EVERY business we passed on the way out had been looted. 

I'll be dealing with insurance etc. for the next few days. I don't have 
regular internet or phone access, but wioll try to read this group when 
I can.
